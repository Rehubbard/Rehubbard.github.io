Frequently Asked Questions

Where are you from?

ANSWER

I was born and raised in Alabaster, AL, a city about 20 miles south of Birmingham.

Roll Tide, right? Since you graduated from The University of Alabama?

ANSWER

It's complicated. I grew up an Auburn fan and then made the wild decision to attend Alabama based on the school's exceptional telecommunications program. So, I'm a fan of both.

What do you like about web development/coding?

ANSWER

I like web development/programming/coding for many reasons. For one, it is challenging and always keeps you on your toes. It is also incredibly rewarding and I feel more valuable as an employee.

What do you like to do in your spare time?

ANSWER

Whether it's playing them or watching them, I'm a huge sports fan. My particular favorites are soccer, basketball, football and hockey. Along with sports, I'm big into video games. I also love being outside and taking my dog with me to explore the beautiful world we live in.

What's your favorite Will Ferrell movie?

ANSWER

Great question. While I love all Will Ferrell movies, my favorite would have to be a tie between Anchorman, Step Brothers, and Semi-Pro.

What would you consider is your best quality?

ANSWER

I consider myself a very determined person. If I set my sights on something, I will not stop until that something is accomplished. I would have to say my relentless drive is my best quality.

In your opinion, what's the best sitcom of all time?

ANSWER

That's a difficult question. If you consider The Office a true sitcom, it would definitely have to be that. However, some people do not consider The Office a true sitcom, and if that's the case I would have to say Friends.
