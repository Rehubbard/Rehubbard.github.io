Eric Hubbard

Developer. Designer. Dreamer.

Ever since I was a kid, I could never keep my hands off technology. I always found myself being amazed by technology and wondering how exactly things worked. I am a web developer with a strong appreciation for lean, efficiently-written code and a particular eye for design. I graduated in May of 2015 from The University of Alabama with a major in advertising and a minor in graphic design. Soon after working in the 'real world', I discovered I belonged on the technological side of advertising. I found I was extremely interested in digital advertising, and this shifted my sights on web development.

I am currently undergoing a web development boot camp hosted by the Depot/U. The rewarding feeling I get after writing successful code is how I know I belong in web development. I consider myself a valuable candidate because I have an eye for design and the ability to code and make things happen.

Take a look around my site, and get in contact with me if you have any questions or comments.
